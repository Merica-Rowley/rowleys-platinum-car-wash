About Us

Family-Owned Car Wash in Washington, Utah

Rowley’s Platinum Car Wash is a family-owned car wash proudly serving Washington, Utah and the surrounding community. Built on the values of hard work, quality, and hometown service, our goal is simple: give every vehicle the care it deserves while making each visit fast, friendly, and dependable. From daily drivers to weekend cruisers, we treat every car like it’s our own.

Part of the Rowley's Red Barn Family

We’re also excited to be part of the Rowley’s Red Barn family, making your stop with us more than just a car wash. Right next to the car wash, you’ll find fresh local produce, homemade bakery items, and sweet treats that make errands a little more enjoyable. Whether you’re grabbing a clean car, freshly baked rolls, or something sweet for the road, Rowley’s Platinum Car Wash and Rowley’s Red Barn are here to serve our community, one smile, one wash, and one great experience at a time.
